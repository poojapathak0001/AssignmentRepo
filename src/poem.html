Mocking Bird

It's been day's, still no word,
Are you lost? Are you hurt?
A whisper is being heard of your fall,
Rumors are finding in their way to crawl.

I know the truth swept you ashore,
But there's always a new tide,so get aboard.
Don't let the demons of past ruin you,
I know you got the light to shoo them,
I know you do.
Don't forget that diamond was but a coal,
A bit of destiny and a lot of hard made it go bold.

You'll get there too, just let the life polish you,
Turn your quest to 'how' and shed 'who'.
Unite with your form, and make your self heard,
Learn and then make it your own, just like the little mocking bird.

Quicksand

"I've killed you a thousand times in my mind,
It's my love that you are alive."
To see love in those lines, I sure was blind,
With the blindfold shreeded, I now prefer hate if your love is that kind.

I let my soul sink in those deep blues,
I'm cursing myself now,
For what I thought was an ocean,
Turned out to be quicksand somehow.

Evil

Call me a sinner.
See for yourself if I care.
Once I’mma start something,
I take it to the end.
Love, hate, fight, regret;
You name it, it’s all covered.
Imperfect version of perfection,
Coz perfect is too overrated.
I let them turn tables thinking it’s their game,
Them hyenas, hungry for the two-second fame.
Coz it hurts more when they lose thinking they are winning,
Nothing is evil with the devils trying to mess with the devil queen.

Tar

I peeled off my skin,
I don’t want your trace on me.
I shaved my head, not a single strand left,
I couldn’t let them neonate touch my face.
I cut through my chest,
Grabbed that heart out,
Shot it at point blank,
It bleed white, don’t you wonder why!
Coz that’s what poison do to you,
exchange its colour with your soul,
Turns all the blood into tar.

Fake-true Love

Like a song I played you,
A song with rythm so divine!
A song I thought I fell in love with,
Until I listened to the lyrics,
It was only your rythm that I liked.

ike a river I flow along you,
A river so serene!
A river I would have let myself drown in,
Until I started sinking in the quicksand,
It was only your tranquil outlier that I liked.

Like a pen I wrote with you,
With ink so dark as if forever it will stay!
A pen I wrote my life with,
Until I turned back the pages and saw,
My life turned into nothingness,
It was only your transient yet beautiful ink that I liked.

Like a memory I cherished you,
A memory I want to freeze in time!
A memory with happiness larger than this universe,
Until I realised it was but my conscience,
It was only my imagination that I mistook for real-time.

Season of Woman

Innocent & naive
like a budding flower,
She's new to this world
as soothing as a blissful shower;
She has a long way to go
& all waiting to see how she grows.

Wow! She has blossomed,
Center of attention, she's the new one.
Full of passion & hot emotions
She's the Goddess of Love Potions.
Lookout for how she dignifies Beauty,
Do watch her glorify bold.

Now she's a native to this land
So calm, serene, mature and tall she stands.
With the petals she shed, she's all fruitful & giving;
She reconciles the blessing, she enjoys the peaceful living.

The fruit is shed, she's all alone,
wrinkles is all that's left, the youhful freckles are gone.
Agony & resentment is enclosing her;
But in this bitter-sweet cold, there's tranquility; she's sure.
Now don't mistake her inactivity for her fall,
For she's looking past it, sowing seed to reborn.

Bloodflower

I hate this place,
And yet I'm here:
Just to see your face.
You were laughing just now with your new love interest,
And now it have already taken pace.

Just to see you suffer,
As much as you made me do,
I'll stick around in your downfall.
Pretend,
as if I'll catch you,
And right before the evil can touch my arms,
I'll let the concrete hit you.
It's not karma,
It's just me: the masochist that you made me be.
I was right, you were a sakura,
A bloodflower,
Only when I touched, the venom spread,
I saw the demon soul mock my chained heart.

Slayer

Little summer dress,
Winter in her heart,
Autumn in the eyes,
There she goes slaying hearts,
My black widow,
Damn she’s fine.
Sarcasm queen.
She ain’t those stereotypes,
Working the money tides,
Perfect balance: body and mind,
Just as hard to get as hard to find.

No matter how hard I try she walks by,
So I stop, rewind.
Pause and play,
And again let her slay.
She got me like,
Stop , rewind, pause & play.

She don’t look eye to eye,
Them other girls,
Copying her style,
Misery.
She’s so fly,
Got me wishing on a wishing well,
To let our stars collide.
Got a billion thoughts,
I wanna say to you,
But words fail to define,
Girl, I’m spell-bound,
Just by the thought of us,
Let commit this perfect crime.
No matter how far I stay, I’m her prey.
So I stop, rewind.
Pause and play,
And again let her slay.
She got me like,
Stop , rewind, pause & play.
